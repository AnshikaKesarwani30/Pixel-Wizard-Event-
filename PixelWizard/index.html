Chicago Jazz Festival

One of the World's Biggest Music Festival

The oldest of Chicago's free lakefront music festivals boasts an incredibly diverse line up, which includes free neighbourhood concerts leading up to the main show at Millennium Park each September. The range of artists comprising the Chicago Jazz Festival runs the gamut from jazz legends to influencial modern masters and crucial new voices in the genre's continuing evolution.
